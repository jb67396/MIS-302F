Who am I?

My name is John Ballock. I am currently a third year Computer Engineering student. I have interest in embedded systems and software Engineering. This past summer I was an intern for Insight Enterprises where I worked in a team dedicated to ensuring smooth system cutovers. The work entailed some work with route switching and network engineering. The previous summer I was an intern for a neurotechnology firm called Brainscope. Here I developed and alternative embedded device to determine eye convergence and accommodation to pre-screen for traumatic brain injuries. I also helped with testing of their electroencephalography devices for concussion detections.
